Brownies

Brownies are chocolate baked products made in square or rectangle shapes. They are commonly consumed around the world and are made with flour, fat (oil or butter), sugar, eggs and chocolate (molten or cocoa powder). Nuts such as walnuts, almonds, pecans and macadamia nuts can be added to provide a characteristic texture to brownies. Common variations of brownies include fudgy brownie, cakey brownie and chewy brownie. The difference between these variations is based on ingredients proportions, the presence of a chemical leavening (e.g baking powder), the type of fat or chocolate, etc.
