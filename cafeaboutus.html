ABOUT US

Established on January 20,2022, Little Syafara Cafe is a cafe equipped with a variety of desserts that can please the heart of a person and their loved ones. Syafara's name was taken with our second name, which is 'Sya' from Syahirah, 'Fa' from Fasihah and 'Ra' from Ameira. While 'Little' means small. When it combined together, it became Little Syafara Cafe which means a small Syafara Cafe. Little Syafara Cafe is a stop over place for many because of its delicious desserts with various types and flavors. In the meantime, Little Syafara Cafe can also cheer up someone who is struck by sadness.
